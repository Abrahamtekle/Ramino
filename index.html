🃏 Ramino – Rules

Setup

  • 2 decks + 4 jokers (108 cards)
  • Anti-clockwise deal · 14 cards each (dealer's right gets 15)

Objective

  • Sets: 3–4 cards same rank
  • Runs: 3+ consecutive same suit
  • Meld all cards to win

51-Point Rule

  • First meld must total ≥ 51 pts
  • A/J/Q/K = 10 · numbers = face value · Jokers = 0

Jokers

  • Wild — represent any card in a meld

Turn

  • 1. Draw from deck or discard
  • 2. Optionally meld (3+ cards)
  • 3. Discard to end turn

Rumino!

  • Meld ALL cards at once before any prior meld = Rumino! — opponents score double
