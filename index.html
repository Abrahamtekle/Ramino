ERITREAN RAMINO

Setup

  • 2 decks + 4 jokers = 108 cards total
  • Deal anti-clockwise
  • Each player gets 14 cards
  • Player to dealer's right gets 15 cards

Objective

  • Get rid of all cards by forming melds
  • Set: 3–4 cards of the same rank (e.g. three Kings)
  • Run: 3+ consecutive cards of the same suit (e.g. 5♥ 6♥ 7♥)

The 51-Point Rule (ሃምሳ ሓደ)

  • Your very first meld must be worth at least 51 points
  • A, J, Q, K = 10 pts · Number cards = face value
  • Jokers count 0 pts toward the 51 rule
  • After your first meld, no minimum required

Jokers (ዋይልድ)

  • Jokers are wild — they can be any card in a meld
  • Max 1 joker per meld

Turn Flow

  • 1. Draw from deck or take the top discard card
  • 2. Optionally lay down melds (select cards → tap Meld)
  • 3. Discard one card to end your turn

Winning

  • First player to empty their hand wins the round
  • Others score penalty points = value of unmelded cards in hand
  • Lowest score after agreed rounds wins the game

RUMINO! ✨ (Special Win)

  • If you meld ALL your cards at once before placing any earlier melds = RUMINO!
  • Opponents score double their deadwood as penalty
  • The rarest and most glorious win!
